About Us

We are an independent, family-owned vehicle customization shop located in Dallas, Texas. At Mach 5 Garage, believe that cars aren't just basic appliances. We believe that cars are not only canvases for their owners to apply their tastes and sensibilites- but also as instruments with limits meant to be pushed, altered, and pushed beyond. At Mach 5 Garage we welcome any customer that shares this vision- no matter how big their garage is.

Why Choose Us?

  • No haggling, no nonsense. The price you see is the price you pay, every time.
  • Bring what you have- we welcome garages of all sizes and have no limit on how many cars one customer can bring in.
  • If you can dream it, we can build it- we can pull from any parts manufacturer you can think of.
  • Beauty isn't just skin deep- we don't just specialize in visual customization, we also install bolt-on performace modifications.
  • Enthusiast owned and operated.
  • Robust inventory management- we're not losing track of any parts that come through our garage door
  • Our skilled craftsman work on any type of car you can bring.
  • Swing by our shop and get a taste of the local car scene.
